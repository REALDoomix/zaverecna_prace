Mechanics of FPS games

The basics of every FPS game is movement and aim. Some games might have extra mechanics, such as: Puzzle,Abilities/Utility,Team play, communication etc. It would take ages to explain every mechanic in FPS games and so I will only describe the core mechanics

Aim: essentials and how to perfect it

Aim is a basic mechanic in every single FPS game, basically you will most probably not succeed at a game unless you can aim well. Games such as CS:GO and Valorant are heavily based on your aiming capability. Aim has multiple parts that you need to perfect.

Software that will improve your aim

There are multiple programs that will let you practice your aim, the most popular are: KovaaKs aim trainer and Aimlab. I will be focusing on Aimlab since it is free, unlike KovaaKs which costs around 10$. There are also multiple ex-professional players that create videos on how to improve. One of the most popular ones is "Ron Rambo Kin", who is an ex-professional CS:GO player. His youtube channel is full of helpful tricks tips & tricks.
